US history

The history of the U.S. is so different than the history of any European country. But one word can describe it: impressive. Because in 17th century, it was nothing there. In 18th, the country was actually born. In 19th - it was growing in the crazy, uncontrolled speed. In 20th - it becames the biggest power in the world, beating Great Britain - their founder and "the best friend". 21th century - it's still the biggest economy, biggest army, and unbeatable in spreading its culture - American songs and movies are present in all houses in almost all the countries in the world which builds the future for this country much more effective than any wars or politics!
